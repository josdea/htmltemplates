Embedded Video

This is text next to a youtube video embedded on the page. On a smaller screen or phone the text on the left will automatically appear first.

You can easily edit the code behind this page and update the video URL as well as the width of the text and video areas by changing the numbers for each. They must add up to 12, currently the text is a 4 and the video is an 8.
